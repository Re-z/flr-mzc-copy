About CFDs

CFDs allow you to start trading without buying or selling physical shares.

This makes trading quicker and easier than ever before. All you need to do is decide whether or not you think the prices will rise or fall. If you invest in the price of Bitcoin rising, you will earn money based on the price difference when it does. The same applies when the price falls! And right now, people are making much more money from price crashes.

Can you really earn money from falling prices?

Yes! CFDs allow you to trade on the price of cryptocurrencies. This means that you can invest in profits and losses. When you invest in a loss, you are saying to the broker that you think the price will go down.

When selling short, you borrow shares at the current price. Let’s say you borrow 100 shares at $1 each. If the price falls to $0.9, when you give the broker back their shares, you give them 100 x $0.9 = $90, and you receive $10. The broker still has all of their shares and you make a profit. It’s as simple as that.

How much money can I earn?

The amount you earn is completely down to you and how much you are willing to invest. The most successful investors are those that reinvest a percentage of their profits. This is because you always stand to earn more the larger your investment. If prices fall by 10% and you invest $100, you’ll receive $10 profit. However, if you invest $1000, you will receive $100.
